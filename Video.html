IQBAL CHILDHOOD

Because of Iqbal Masih's extreme curiosity, caring selflessness, and steadfast determination, he was able to escape the cruelty of his child bondage, save the lives of thousands of children around the globe, and inspire many others to take up his cause and passion. His curiosity allowed for him to learn much in life, meet many of his goals, and see the possibility for change. With his slogan "A Bullet Can't Kill A Dream" ringing true today, Iqbal's ability to live on through the actions of others and continue to power his cause even after death put together with his qualities of inquisitiveness, compassion, and determination label him as a true hero.
